When you or an ally rolls for an Anomaly ability other than this one, after seeing the result, punch a clock and roll **Initiative**.
## On a Success,
you send the target back in time to assist themselves, allowing them to use the same ability a second time with an identical number of 3s. This second use can affect new targets and does not generate Chaos or Triscendence effects.
## For each additional 3,
you add one 3 to the second use. This effect can make a roll exceed six 3s.
## On a failure,
the original roll becomes a failure, and then the ability fails a second time. The second failure repeats on the same target or moves to a new target, whichever is worse. Chaos is not generated by the copied failure.
