Tune yourself to any frequency. Touch anything and roll **Attentiveness**

## On a success,
you tune yourself to the frequency of the touched item or substance, and are able to identify any instances of it within the distance a scream would be autidble (a few blocks in a city, farther in a rural area, very far over still water, etc) You are able to roughly determine the mass and location of the others, as well as their speed and direction. This effect lasts for as long as you are touching the thing.

## On every third 3, you may choose one:
- You may treat all of the items detected this way as a speaker, and the thing you're touching as a microphone
- You may stop the motion of all instances of the item or substance by putting pressure on the one you're touching, bringing all to an immediate halt. If they were making noise, they cease to do so until the effect ends.
- You may jostly all of the things detected this way by rapidly shaking the one you're touching. The affected item moves in proportion to its size: a baseball might move wildly while a building may only move imperceptibly

## On a failure,
you tune yourself to every frequency. The GM selects one of the "On every third 3" effects and everything within the ability's range experiences that effect until you exit the affected area.
