# Description:
Voyagers from Heroia. Video game characters from a fantasy game that escaped the game and are trying to live in the real world.
# Connection Level: 6

# Anomaly Ability: Side Quest
Ask a bystander for an object you seek. Say to someone: "Hail and well met. Do you know where I can find..." and then an object you seek. Roll **Presence**

## On a success,
the target has knowledge of, access to, or possession of the sought object - you choose. The target will trade it to you in exchange for 9 of something you don't currently have, but have seen earlier today (bus tickets, chocolate cupcakes, photographs of men named charles, etc.). You GM will choose

## For each additional 3,
the target requires one less of the thing you don't currently have, to a minimum of zero.

## On a failure,
the target has no knowledge of, access to, or possession of the sought object. They loudly refuse to leave your side until after you have collected 100 of something you don't currently have, but have seen earlier today. Your GM will choose.
